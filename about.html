About us? What about us!?
Our community what we're based around. Everything we do is for the community, but what exactly we do do...?
We have many goals... the first one?
We want to provide information for everyone. We don't care who you are or what you do, we should all have the same information
Once we can do this... guess we'll have done our job.
And for our second goal?
We seek to help further communities. Not everyone has a fair chance to be successful, it's all about size these days...
So what if we can change things?
Time for our third.. and final goal.
Uniting people. Around the world people lack resources of many kinds, even simple things like a place to hang out.
Bringing these resources to people... the goal.
